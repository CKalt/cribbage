# prompts/ai-rich-celebrations-sound-design-plan.md

## Goal

Design subtle, tasteful sound cues to complement celebrations.

No casino noise. No arcade effects. Wood. Cards. Subtlety.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Sound Palette

-   Soft wooden peg click
-   Light card shuffle whisper
-   Gentle coin-like tap for 31
-   Quiet confetti rustle
-   Subtle "ding" for 24+
-   Low warm tone for win
-   Brief muted thump for skunk

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Intensity Mapping

Low: - Soft peg click

Medium: - Peg click + faint shimmer

High: - Shimmer + soft celebratory chord

Legendary: - Very brief warm harmonic swell (under 1 second)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Implementation Notes

-   Use small \<20KB audio files
-   Preload after first interaction
-   Respect mute settings
-   Separate Sound Level setting:
    -   Off
    -   Subtle
    -   Standard

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Accessibility

-   Always optional
-   No critical gameplay tied to sound
-   No sudden loud spikes
-   Respect system volume

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Design Principle

Sound should feel like: - A peg tapping wood - Cards sliding on felt - A
quiet chuckle across the table

Not like a slot machine.
