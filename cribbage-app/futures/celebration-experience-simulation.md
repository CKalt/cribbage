# docs/celebration-experience-simulation.md

## Purpose

Simulate how 10 consecutive hands feel under:

-   Classic
-   Lively
-   Full Banter

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Scenario: Mixed 10-Hand Stretch

Hands: 1. 8 points 2. 0 points 3. 20 points 4. Peg 31 5. 12 points 6. 24
points 7. Opponent 18 8. Go steal 9. 16 points 10. Close win by 3

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Classic Mode

-   Subtle glow on 20+
-   Toast for 24
-   Understated phrase for zero
-   Small sparkle on 31
-   Close win phrase
-   No overwhelming animations

Feels tasteful and table-authentic.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Lively Mode

-   Toast for 20+
-   Sparkle + wobble for 24
-   Peg 31 bounce
-   Go steal toast
-   Close win mini-confetti
-   Moderate animation variety

Feels energetic but controlled.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Full Banter Mode

-   Big phrases for 20+
-   Maine Lodge phrases sprinkled in
-   Mini-confetti for 24
-   Speech bubble pop
-   Close win + playful taunt
-   Occasional streak escalation

Feels alive, humorous, and social.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Key Insight

Classic feels like cribbage.

Lively feels like friendly competition.

Full Banter feels like Friday night at the lodge.
