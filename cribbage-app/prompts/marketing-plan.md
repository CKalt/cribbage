## Overview

Cribbage at [cribbage.chrisk.com](https://cribbage.chrisk.com) is a polished single-player cribbage game with Expert Mode AI, muggins rules, and a leaderboard. The current user base is small but engaged â€” notably Shawn, who has played 500+ games.

This plan outlines a strategy to grow membership by leveraging the existing product strengths, building community features around the upcoming multiplayer mode, and positioning the app within the broader **SKnowball** ecosystem.

The core thesis: **cribbage players are community-oriented by nature** â€” they play at kitchen tables, in pubs, and in clubs. An online cribbage community that captures that social feel will grow through word of mouth if we give players reasons to invite their friends.

## Problem Statement

1. **Discovery**: Players don't know we exist. There is no organic acquisition channel.
2. **Retention hook**: Single-player is engaging but finite â€” once you've beaten the AI, what's next?
3. **Social loop**: There is no mechanism for players to bring their friends into the app.
4. **Community identity**: The app is a standalone game, not yet part of a larger story that gives players a sense of belonging.

The multiplayer mode (currently in beta) and the **SKnowball** umbrella will address all four.

## Phase 1: Foundation â€” Polish the Single-Player Experience

*Goal: Make the current product impressive enough that players want to share it.*

### 1.1 Expert Mode Launch Announcement

- [ ] Craft a short email / in-app announcement to existing users about Expert Mode
